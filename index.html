About KB Design

KB Design was founded in 2011 by Kiri Bermack. Kiri Bermack began her career as a fine artist and starteddesigning light sculptures in 2001.

As a fine artist, Kiri has competed andbeen selected to exhibit her work inseveral juried art shows in New York City.
In 1998,1,000 artists competed in thePleiades Gallery exhibition and Kiri's clay sculpture"Scars' was one of the 89 pieces selected by Juror,Ivan Karp from O.K. Harris gallery.
In 2001 & 2003, Kiri's light sculptureswere exhibited at the Form & Function Showsat the Denise Bibro Fine Art gallery.
In 2005,her postcard design was selectedat the "Wish You Were Here" exhibitionat the A.I.R. gallery.
In 2009, one of Kiri's bicycle wheelsculptures was selected by juror Daniel Ferris,Director of the Stephen Haller Gallery,for the "Small Works Show" atWashington Square East Galleryat New York University.

Our Services

Experience our high-energy lighting design and installation services tailored to your needs.
Our lights intensify the feeling of each color. Using light and color, designs are customized to the aesthetics and spiritual needs of each client.
Our lighting can also be holistic. Like the ancient Egyptians and Chinese, we use color for healing. We work with chroma therapists to select colors for our designs.
KB Design supports the "Going Green" movement. Most of the materials are recyclable.
